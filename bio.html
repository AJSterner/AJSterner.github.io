Andrew Sterner was born in Berkeley, California. He spend the first six years of his life in Richmond, CA before moving to Albany, CA where he spent his remaining childhood in public school. Graduating Albany High School in 2015, he went on to Tufts University where he currently studies Computer Science and Economics. He enjoys Snowboarding, food, coffee and long walks in the rain
